- Who Am I? -

I am a Front-End Developer and Graphic Designer with a passion for responsive and intuitive UI/UX layouts. I enjoy the challenge of building something that truly represents the client image and mission statement. My background includes a vast array of skills that aide in my development strategy including sales, marketing, and brand activation. I incorporate that knowledge into every design in order to keep my work not only visually appealing, but functional.

When I am not designing or developing content I am spending time with my family or riding motorcycles with my friends. I've always been known as the guy with the camera, so even when I'm not working I'm still creating. A great deal of my time is spent outdoors, enjoying family activities, and nature.
